SHEETAL BAMRAH

Sheetal Bamrah is a professional hair and makeup artist known for creating refined soft-glam looks that elevate natural beauty with elegance and precision. With a background in science, she brings a thoughtful and detail-oriented approach to her artistry, carefully selecting techniques and products that complement each client's unique features.

Her work focuses on enhancing — not transforming — so that each look remains authentic, luminous, and timeless in both person and photograph.

Through Sheetal Bamrah Artistry, she is dedicated to delivering a luxury beauty experience rooted in professionalism, artistry, and genuine care for every bride and client she serves.
